2003

2006
	**April:** 
		*Dave gives Astralis a cheap apartment.* He offers to pay it fully, but in an attempt to show that she is capable she refuses and tries to pay rent
2007
	**August:**
		*Astralis turns 21.* On her birthday she gets drunk for the first time by herself, alone, in her empty apartment. She calls Joseph, drunkenly flirts. He comes over to check that she's okay and after talking Astralis kisses him. He helps her into bed, ignoring her remarks about how he should join her, and goes to sit on her sofa to think, leaving before she wakes up. Astralis doesn't remember.
	**November:**
		*Joseph breaks and tells Annalise that Astralis kissed him.* Annalise breaks up with, and kicks Joseph out.
		*Joseph "temporarily" moves in with Astralis.* Joseph stays on the sofa
	**December:**
		*Joseph and Astralis start dating*. After helping Joseph through the break up, they end up sleeping together
2008
	**January:**
		*Eris and Sage get engaged.* Astralis sees a post about it on myspace captioned "I said yes! Here's to the rest of our lives together My Evergreen. If only Skye could see us now..." Astralis reacts poorly to this, ends up throwing a vase at the wall as Joseph tries to calm her down
	**February:**
		*Joseph talks to Dave about Astralis' outburst.* Suggests talking with her about therapy which Dave ends up paying for. Astralis is not happy about this.
		*Astralis gets medicated and starts to slowly process...everything*
	**March:**
		*Astralis and Joseph move to a slightly bigger apartment...also owned by Dave Honeyfinger.* This is somewhat of a present for Astralis getting more stable on her meds
	**April:**
		*Joseph starts HRT!!* He has to go to Sage for this so wait until he thinks Astralis can handle that
		*Tassa moves in with Joseph and Astralis.* With Astra and Joseph both having to pay for different kinds of medication, rent costs are harder to meet. Astralis gets the bright idea to invite her ex to stay with them.
2010
	**June:**
		*Eris and Sage get married.* Astralis is invited as a bridesmaid, during the ceremony it becomes apparent that she is there as a stand-in for Skye. This causes her to storm off home and in a fit of rage she flushes her meds down the toilet. Starts quickly declining. Joseph gently confronts her one night about it, reasonably suggesting she go back to therapy and gets more medication. Unreasonably, she throws a lamp at his head causing him to need stitches
	**July:**
		*Astralis attempts suicide for the second time.* After dealing with the anger from the wedding and the guilt of assaulting Joseph she takes a lot of various pills in the bathroom cabinet. Over the next few hours she gets sicker until collapsing. Joseph calls an ambulance and then Dave and Pastellion when she is stable
		*Dave gets Astralis admitted.* Doing so after seeing how therapy and medication didn't work. Joseph visits as often as possible and encourages her to engage with the processes. Astralis feels very pitied and ashamed during this time. Pastellion visits her semi-frequently as well, preaching to her.
	**September:**
		*Astralis is released*
	**November**
		*Joseph proposes to Astralis.* Once he is sure that she is more stable-er-ish (not gonna throw anymore lamps), they get engaged.
2011
	**December:**
		*Dave Honeyfinger is murdered.* Astralis is distraught until Elsie gets her to reframe her perspective as not a victim, but instead someone to solve it. She does so unhealthily
2012
	**October:**
		*Elsie March is murdered.* Due to her investigation into Dave's murder. Astralis notices similar evidence linking the two and starts to look deeper
	**December:**
		*Astralis has to go into witness protection.* She digs too deep into the investigation and ends up in the witness protection program for 9 months while the police look into the ring of organised crime she accidentally unearthed. She can't see anyone during this time.
2013
	**August:**
		*Astralis is given the all clear to move back home.* She soon rejoins the church to pray for her loved ones safety, as well as to feel more connected to Joseph now she's back
2014
	**March**
		*Astralis cheats on Joseph with Ardinys.* After Astralis discovers a tracking app on her phone that Joseph hid from her, they fight. After which Astralis storms out to go and drink at Buddy's a rotating gay bar in town. There she runs into, after 10 years, Ardinys Antares. They sleep together. Astralis lets her move into the old apartment she now owns and gives Ardi money to help her get back on her feet
